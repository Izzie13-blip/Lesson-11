Art

Origin The earliest doodles are believed to be cave markings made by our ancestors around 500,000 years ago. The world's oldest known doodle is red lines on a rock that are 73,000 years old.



Purpose Doodling can serve many purposes, including storytelling, record-keeping, and expressing reverence



Styles There are many different styles of doodle art, including: Zentangle, which involves creating structured patterns within a defined space Mandala Cartooning Lettering Abstract doodling





Benefits Doodling can help people: Remember information Fill in gaps in their thinking Take a break from a lot of information Come up with ideas to solve problems
